### Rules
- A Subscription is optional
- A User may subscribe to a Commitment without posting
- A User may post to a Commitment without subscribing

### Relationships
- A Subscription links exactly one User to one Commitment

### Notes
- Subscriptions do not grant posting permissions
- Subscriptions do not restrict posting permissions
- Subscriptions exist primarily for notifications and feed visibility
