### Overview
A Commitment represents a pledge made by a User.

## Properties

- [[Post|Posts]]
- [[Requirement|Requirements]] 
### Rules
- A Commitment has exactly one owner
- A Commitment may have zero or more Requirements
- A Commitment may have zero or more Posts
- A Commitment may have zero or more Subscriptions

### Relationships
- A Commitment belongs to exactly one User (the owner)
- A Commitment may be posted to by any User
- Any User may post on a Commitment.
