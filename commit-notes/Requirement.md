## Overview
A Requirement defines a rule that must be satisfied for a Commitment
to be considered "kept".

## Properties
- [[Post Frequency]]
- [[Image]]
- [[Text Update]]

### Rules
- A Requirement belongs to exactly one Commitment
- Requirements are evaluated only against check-in Posts
- Only check-in Posts created by the Commitment owner are evaluated

### Notes
- Requirements define constraints, not content
- Requirements do not apply to comment Posts
